Welcome to PROHIBIDO,

We propose you to live a unique experience around the Prohibition of the Twenties, when jazz and the underground bars, "Speakeasy", would proliferate in New York.

The phrase "Speakeasy" originated in the Bartenders' habit of asking their customers to place their orders discreetly, in order not to draw attention to an era where the sale of alcohol was prohibited to United States.

All the Prohibido team is ready to welcome you in the rules of the art, from Thursday to Saturday evenings from 6pm.

But... Be discreet...
